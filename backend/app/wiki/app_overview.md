# What Does This App Do

## Overview
This is a conversational recruiting analytics AI assistant. It lets recruiters, hiring managers, and HR admins ask natural language questions about their hiring data and get instant, formatted answers — without needing to log into multiple systems or run manual reports.

Instead of navigating separate tools for requisitions, candidate pipelines, headcount, and interview schedules, you simply type a question and the AI figures out which data to fetch, queries the right systems, and presents the answer in plain language.

## Who Uses It
- **Recruiters** — track pipeline health, candidate stages, sourcing mix, and req status across all orgs
- **Hiring Managers** — see their own open reqs, candidates, and pending starts
- **HR Admins** — view org-wide headcount, attrition, hiring velocity, and plan attainment

## What You Can Ask
- How many open reqs do we have by org or location?
- Which candidates are closest to an offer?
- What is the offer acceptance rate trend this year?
- Are there any onboarding risks for pending starts?
- Which orgs are behind on their hiring plan?
- Show me the Bar Raiser interview schedule for this week
- What is the average time to hire for SDE roles?
- Give me a full recruiting health check for a specific org

## How It Works
The AI reads your question, looks up the relevant internal APIs, fetches the data, and writes a recruiter-friendly summary. For complex questions spanning multiple data sources, it queries all the systems simultaneously and combines the results.

## Data Freshness
All data shown reflects the current state of the underlying recruiting systems at the time of the query.

## Roles and Permissions
Access is controlled by your login role. Hiring managers only see their own requisitions and candidates. Recruiters and admins see data across all orgs.
